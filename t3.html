Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices of bread
  • butter, as needed

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy, then set aside.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place two slices of bread, butter-side down, in the pan.
  4. Add a slice of cheese and some tomato slices to each sandwich.
  5. Top with the cooked bacon and the remaining cheese.
  6. Place the remaining slices of bread, butter-side up, on top of the sandwiches.
  7. Cook over medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
